NSTU Officers Association-2022

Noakhali Science and Technology University (NSTU) Officers Association Election 2021 has been held. Mr. Sakhawat Hossain was elected president and Mr. Mejbah Uddin Palash was elected general secretary. Today Sunday (December 19, 2021) polling was held from 10.00 am to 2.30 pm in the presence of Honorable Vice-Chancellor of NSTU, Prof. Dr.Md. Didar-ul-Alam. In the presence of Chief Election Commissioner Engineer Md. Jamal Hossain, Honorable Vice-Chancellor of NSTU, Md. Didar-ul-Alam announced the result.The treasurer, the president of the teacher's association, Registrar (Additional Responsibility), Deputy Controller of Examinations, Deputy Registrar (Administration) was also present.
